## 💡 核心使命 | Mission
Traditional AI video generation relies on "probabilistic guessing," leading to flickering objects and broken physical logic. SLA 1.0 enforces **Physical Sovereignty**, shifting the AI paradigm from pixel-based generation to **Deterministic Logic Segments**.

传统的 AI 视频生成依赖“概率猜测”，导致物体闪烁与物理逻辑断裂。SLA 1.0 强制执行**“物理主权”**，将 AI 生成范式从像素概率转向**确定性的逻辑段落**。

## 🛠️ 核心架构 | Core Architecture

### 1. Viewport-Driven Collapse (视口驱动环境坍缩)
* **Logic**: The observer's POV coordinates define the environment's state.
* **Impact**: Eliminates spatial jitter and ensures environmental consistency during camera movement.
* **逻辑**: 由观测者视角坐标定义环境状态，根治运镜过程中的空间闪烁。

### 2. Segment Transport Logic (特征段落搬运)
* **Logic**: Mathematical "Copy-Paste" of physical features for reflections and shadows.
* **Impact**: Guarantees 100% optical consistency in mirrors and water surfaces.
* **逻辑**: 物理特征在反射与阴影中的数学级“复印搬运”，确保镜像与水面的绝对物理一致。

### 3. CN-Material Spec Container (中式材质物理容器)
* **Standard**: Defines industrial parameters for **Vermilion Lacquer, Grey Tiles, and Mortise-Tenon joints**.
* **Impact**: Restores the structural integrity of Eastern architecture in AI rendering.
* **标准**: 确立**朱漆、黛瓦、隼卯结构**的工业级参数，还原中式建筑的结构美学主权。
