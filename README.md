## 💡 核心使命 | Mission
Traditional AI video generation relies on "probabilistic guessing," leading to flickering objects and broken physical logic. SLA 1.0 enforces **Universal Physical Sovereignty**, shifting the AI paradigm from pixel-based generation to **Deterministic Logic Segments**.

传统的 AI 视频生成依赖“概率猜测”，导致物体闪烁与物理逻辑断裂。SLA 1.0 强制执行**“全球物理主权”**，将 AI 生成范式从像素概率转向**确定性的逻辑段落**。

## 🛠️ 核心架构 | Core Architecture (Global Applicability)

### 1. Viewport-Driven Collapse (视口驱动环境坍缩)
* **Logic**: The observer's POV coordinates define the environment's state. 
* **Global Use**: Applicable to any 3D scene (Urban, Nature, Sci-Fi) to ensure spatial consistency.
* **逻辑**: 由观测者视角坐标定义环境状态。适用于任何 3D 场景（城市、自然、科幻），根治空间闪烁。

### 2. Segment Transport Logic (特征段落搬运)
* **Logic**: Mathematical "Copy-Paste" of physical features for reflections and shadows.
* **Global Use**: Guarantees 100% optical consistency across all reflective surfaces globally.
* **逻辑**: 物理特征在反射与阴影中的数学级“复印搬运”，确保全球任何反射表面的绝对物理一致。

### 3. Multi-Scenario Material Specs (多场景材质规范容器)
* **Initial Standard**: **CN-Material Specs** (Vermilion Lacquer, Grey Tiles, Mortise-Tenon joints).
* **Extensibility**: Framework supports global architectural and natural material parameters.
* **标准**: 首发包含**中式材质规范**（朱漆、黛瓦、隼卯）。框架具备全球通用性，支持各类建筑与自然材质参数扩展。
